Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and letting you manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use standard security practices to protect it from unauthorised access. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and not use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond as soon as possible.
